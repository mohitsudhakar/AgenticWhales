🐳 AgenticWhales

Your personal AI hedge fund.

A pod of agents researches every ticker, weighs the same external signals a real desk watches, paper-trades the verdict, and keeps a cognitive journal on your behaviour — all under hard risk limits. Sign in to step onto the floor.

01

A pod of agents puts every ticker on trial.

Market, news, quant and fundamental analysts each file a brief. A bull and a bear argue it out, the risk team weighs in, and a Portfolio Manager hands down a structured BUY / SELL / HOLD — in under two minutes, with a glass-box transcript you can replay agent by agent.

02

The same signals a real desk watches.

The pod doesn't argue in a vacuum. It folds in the external evidence that moves markets — disclosed Congressional trades, influencer calls pulled from X, and your own historical trade record — so every verdict is grounded in what insiders, the crowd, and your past self are actually doing.

03

A paper book that keeps score.

Every decision becomes a paper trade, sized by the risk engine. Positions, orders, realised and unrealised P&L update live, and each closed trade is scored for calibration with a Brier metric — so the fund builds an honest, measurable track record. No real broker, no real money — yet.

04

Persistent recipes run the floor 24/7.

Describe a thesis in plain English — "if SMCI breaks $1,200 on more than 2× average volume, fade it." The strategy agent turns it into rules, the scheduler watches the tape, and the pod convenes a fresh debate the moment conditions converge — even while you sleep.

05

A cognitive journal, risk-first by design.

A behavioural journal flags your tilt, revenge trades and drift, and answers plain-English questions about your own history. Underneath it all: daily spend caps, drawdown rules, per-trade exposure limits and a hard kill switch. The Risk agent has veto power over the PM — discipline is the default.

Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-05-04

AgenticWhales is a multi-agent trading research tool. This page explains what data we collect and why.

What we store

  • Account data — if you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and Google account ID. We use these to identify you across sessions and to attribute analyses to your account.
  • Usage counters — we record how many analyses you've started today, scoped per user. This is what powers the daily quota for free (Novice) accounts.
  • Tier & preferences — your chosen display name and your subscription tier (Novice / Intermediate / Master).
  • Analysis history — the configuration and outputs of analyses you run are stored locally on the server you deploy AgenticWhales to. They are not transmitted to AgenticWhales operators.

What we do NOT store

  • We do not store your Google password — sign-in is delegated to Google.
  • We do not collect payment information until you explicitly subscribe to a paid tier.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

Third parties

Authentication and the per-day usage counter are stored in Supabase (Auth + Postgres). Supabase's own privacy terms apply to data they receive on our behalf. Google Sign-In is delegated to Google as the OAuth identity provider.

Your rights

You can sign out at any time from the sidebar. To delete your account or your stored usage data, contact the operator of this AgenticWhales deployment.

Disclaimer

AgenticWhales is a research tool. Outputs are not financial, investment, or trading advice. You are solely responsible for any decisions you make using this software.
