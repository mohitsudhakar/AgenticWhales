The decision pipeline

A trading desk's workflow, run by agents.

Every analysis flows through the same committee a real fund uses — but the soft, persuadable layer never touches the money math. The Portfolio Manager emits a structured decision; deterministic risk and sizing logic does the rest.

01 · ANALYSTS

Put it on trial

Market, news, quant & fundamental analysts each file an evidence-backed brief.

02 · DEBATE

Bull vs. bear

Two researchers argue opposite sides — drawn from different model families to avoid a united front.

03 · RISK

The risk committee

Aggressive, conservative & neutral voices stress-test the trade before it's sized.

04 · VERDICT

Structured decision

The PM hands down a rating, conviction, expected return & probability — not prose.

05 · LEARN

Size, fill, grade

Fractional-Kelly sizing under hard limits, a paper fill, then Brier-scored against reality.
