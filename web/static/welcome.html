The decision pipeline

A trading desk's workflow, run by agents.

Every analysis flows through the same committee a real fund uses — but the soft, persuadable layer never touches the money math. The portfolio manager emits a structured decision; deterministic risk and sizing logic does the rest.

Why it's different

Discipline is the product.

Anyone can ask a model for a stock pick. AgenticWhales is the system around the model — the risk spine, the audit trail, and the feedback loop that compound into an edge.

Hard risk limits

Position caps, daily-drawdown halts, slippage bounds and kill switches bound every trade — autonomous or manual. The model never overrides them.

Deterministic sizing

Fractional-Kelly position sizing, calibrated to the system's own track record. No vibes — auditable math you can replay.

A cognitive journal

It keeps a journal on your behaviour too — flagging tilt, revenge trades and overconfidence before they cost you.

The signals a desk watches

News, congressional disclosures, social flow, fundamentals and technicals — folded into one verdict, with provenance.

It learns

Every resolved outcome feeds calibration, memory and prompt evaluation — so stated probabilities track reality over time.

Reproducible by design

Every decision can be replayed from the exact data, model and prompt it saw. Performance you can verify, not just claim.

Built for people who live in markets.

Whether you run a book at a multi-manager fund or simply take your own capital seriously, AgenticWhales gives you a tireless research committee and a disciplined risk process.

  • Spin up recurring recipes that re-analyse your watchlist on a schedule or a market event.
  • Paper-trade the verdicts and watch a calibrated track record build, name by name.
  • Ask the fund why it did what it did — every call is logged, attributed and scored.

Where this is going

The goal is a fully agentic hedge fund — one that runs itself.

Today it researches, sizes, and paper-trades under your eye. The arc is an autonomous fund that sources its own data, constructs and rebalances a portfolio across positions, learns continuously from its own track record, and explains every move — operated in plain language, bounded by hard risk limits you set.

The principles never move: the model proposes, the risk engine disposes — sizing, limits, and execution stay deterministic and auditable, and live capital is always an explicit, gated step.

AgenticWhales is a research and education tool. It executes paper trades only — no real orders are placed and no money is moved. Nothing here is financial, investment or trading advice. Markets involve risk; past simulated performance does not predict future results.
