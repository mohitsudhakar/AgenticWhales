      Agent

      🐳 AgenticWhales

      Welcome aboard the trading floor.

      A pod of AI analysts, researchers, traders and risk managers debating every ticker — so you don't have to.

      01

      Pick a ticker, set a depth.

      You configure a run: an instrument (or a basket), an analysis date, which analyst desks to involve, and how many rounds of debate the researchers should hold.

      02

      Analysts gather the evidence.

      Specialised LLM agents pull market data, news, social sentiment and fundamentals in parallel — each producing a written brief that the rest of the team can read.

      03

      Researchers debate, bull vs. bear.

      A bull and a bear researcher argue the position over multiple rounds. A research manager then summarises the strongest case on each side.

      04

      Trader proposes, Risk challenges.

      The trader drafts an actionable plan. A risk team — aggressive, conservative and neutral — pressure-tests it from three different angles before it goes upstairs.

      05

      Portfolio Manager makes the call.

      The PM reads everything, weighs it against your existing positions if you've shared them, and issues the final BUY / SELL / HOLD with rationale you can audit, agent by agent.

      Privacy Policy

      Effective: 2026-05-04

      AgenticWhales is a multi-agent trading research tool. This page explains what data we collect and why.

      What we store

      • Account data — if you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and Google account ID. We use these to identify you across sessions and to attribute analyses to your account.
      • Usage counters — we record how many analyses you've started today, scoped per user. This is what powers the daily quota for free (Novice) accounts.
      • Tier & preferences — your chosen display name and your subscription tier (Novice / Intermediate / Master).
      • Analysis history — the configuration and outputs of analyses you run are stored locally on the server you deploy AgenticWhales to. They are not transmitted to AgenticWhales operators.

      What we do NOT store

      • We do not store your Google password — sign-in is delegated to Google.
      • We do not collect payment information until you explicitly subscribe to a paid tier.
      • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

      Third parties

      Authentication and the per-day usage counter are stored in Supabase (Auth + Postgres). Supabase's own privacy terms apply to data they receive on our behalf. Google Sign-In is delegated to Google as the OAuth identity provider.

      Your rights

      You can sign out at any time from the sidebar. To delete your account or your stored usage data, contact the operator of this AgenticWhales deployment.

      Disclaimer

      AgenticWhales is a research tool. Outputs are not financial, investment, or trading advice. You are solely responsible for any decisions you make using this software.
