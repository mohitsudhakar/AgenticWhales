    Kill switch

    Block all new paper orders
    Toggle on to halt every order regardless of policy. Existing positions are untouched.

    Behavioral cooldown

    Block next order after a tilt / revenge finding
    Detection always runs. This toggle decides whether the fund acts on it. When on, the next paper order within 60min of a flagged pattern is blocked with a tilt_cooldown risk event.

    X Trade Recommendations

    Enter an X (Twitter) handle. Their recent posts are pulled via the X API and an LLM extracts any explicit trade recommendations (ticker · action · conviction · timeframe · rationale). Self-reported opinion, not advice.

    Congressional Trade Analyzer

    Disclosed U.S. House & Senate stock transactions for a ticker. Source: QuiverQuant's aggregation of the official House Clerk / Senate financial disclosures. Disclosure data — not advice.

    Your Trade History Analyzer

    Upload a brokerage CSV (Robinhood / Schwab / Fidelity / generic). A deterministic FIFO engine computes realized PnL, behavioral flags, monthly activity, and per-symbol stats. Optional LLM review gives a 4-lens (risk / discipline / diversification / habits) read on your patterns. Files are processed in-memory; nothing is persisted to disk.
